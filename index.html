Research

（把下面每个方向的描述换成你自己的 3–5 句话就很强了）

1) On-chip Terahertz Spectroscopy Platforms

Broadband on-chip THz generation/detection, impedance-aware device coupling, calibration & de-embedding, and engineering-friendly measurement workflows for quantum devices.

  • Waveguide / antenna coupling and device-compatible geometries
  • System response calibration, deconvolution, and reproducible pipelines
  • Compatible with cryogenic & magnet environments

2) Correlated Electrodynamics & Superconductivity

THz conductivity as a direct probe of low-energy excitations, superfluid density, scattering, and gap dynamics in unconventional superconductors and correlated 2D systems.

  • Complex conductivity σ(ω) = σ₁ + iσ₂
  • Superfluid spectral weight, pairing symmetry fingerprints
  • Nonequilibrium dynamics and recovery pathways

3) Ultrafast Carrier & Heat Transport Dynamics

Multi-timescale dynamics from femtoseconds to nanoseconds: hot carriers, lattice heating, and heat dissipation shaping transient transport.

  • THz/optical pump–probe, time-resolved conductivity
  • Photo-thermal coupling and transport regulation
  • Materials: MXenes, TMDs, vdW heterostructures

4) Quantum Transport in Low-Dimensional Devices

Gate-tunable transport, contact engineering, and device physics. THz spectroscopy complements DC transport by accessing intrinsic electrodynamics at meV energies.

  • 2D devices, moiré superlattices, interface engineering
  • High-field / low-temperature measurement strategies
  • Bridging THz and DC transport observables
