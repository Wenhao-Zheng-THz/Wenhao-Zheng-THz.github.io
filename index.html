Ultrafast Terahertz Electrodynamics of Quantum Materials group

We are an interdisciplinary research group dedicated to quantum physics and advanced spectroscopy. By bridging quantum materials, ultrafast optics, and chip-scale electronics, we develop state-of-the-art THz platforms to probe the hidden dynamics of low-dimensional systems. Our mission is to uncover the fundamental physics governing low-energy electrodynamics, many-body correlations, and non-equilibrium transport in condensed matter.

Our lab is based at the Aerospace Information Research Institute, Chinese Academy of Sciences (GBA branch), Guangzhou. We welcome inquiries from candidates interested in pursuing opportunities as Bachelor’s students, Master’s students, PhD students, Postdoctoral researchers, Engineers, and Assistant Professors. If you are motivated to explore ultrafast spectroscopy, low-dimensional (2D) materials, and low-temperature quantum transport, please feel free to contact me at: thz_zheng@163.com.
