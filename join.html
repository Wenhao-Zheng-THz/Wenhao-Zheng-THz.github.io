Join Us

We are building an open, collaborative lab focused on ultrafast terahertz electrodynamics and on-chip THz platforms. If you’re curious about quantum materials and enjoy learning by doing, we’d be happy to connect.
A lab is a team — we grow together.
We care about a supportive environment, clear communication, and steady progress. You don’t need to “know everything” on day one — what matters most is curiosity, kindness, and the willingness to learn and build things together.

Open Roles

  • PhD / Master Students: ultrafast optics, THz spectroscopy, on-chip platforms, 2D quantum materials, low-temperature transport
  • Postdoctoral Researchers: THz/ultrafast, device physics, correlated states & superconducting electrodynamics
  • Engineers / Research Assistants: system building, automation & control, data pipelines, measurement workflows

How We Work

  • Mentorship & co-working: we solve problems side-by-side and share know-how openly
  • Reproducible research: careful notes, clean data, and results we can trust
  • Respectful collaboration: we value honest discussion and helping each other succeed

Good Fit If You Like…

  • Building platforms (optics/THz/cryogenics/electronics) and seeing them come to life
  • Hard measurements with real-world imperfections—and learning to make them robust
  • Turning data into physics through thoughtful analysis and discussion
