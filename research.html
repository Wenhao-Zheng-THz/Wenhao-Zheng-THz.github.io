Research

We develop ultrafast terahertz spectroscopy and on-chip THz platforms to understand low-energy electrodynamics and transport in quantum materials.
Two complementary research directions
We build experimental capabilities and use them to probe nonequilibrium dynamics and correlated electronic states.
Direction 1

Ultrafast optical/THz pump-probe spectroscopy: nonequilibrium carrier dynamics

Using pump–probe techniques to resolve transient charge dynamics and energy flow following photoexcitation.

  • Transient photoconductivity
  • Many-body interaction (carrier-phonon, electron-hole)
Ultrafast pump–THz spectroscopy
Direction 2

Cryogenic transport & on-chip THz spectroscopy: correlated quantum states

Combining low-temperature transport and integrated THz spectroscopy to investigate correlated electronic states.

  • Complex conductivity σ(ω)
  • Field & temperature dependent THz response
  • On-chip THz platforms
On-chip THz spectroscopy
